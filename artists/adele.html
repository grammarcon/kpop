Adele

Artist Profile

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins MBE (born May 5, 1988) is a British singer-songwriter known for her powerful vocals and emotional ballads. Her music combines elements of soul, R&B, and pop, creating a unique sound that has captivated audiences worldwide.
