Shakira

Global Latin Pop Icon

Artist Profile

Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll (born February 2, 1977) is a Colombian singer-songwriter known for her unique voice, belly dancing, and fusion of Latin, rock, and Arabic musical styles. She is one of the most successful Latin artists of all time.
