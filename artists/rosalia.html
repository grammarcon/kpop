Rosalía

Flamenco Fusion Artist

Artist Profile

Rosalía Vila Tobella (born September 25, 1992) is a Spanish singer-songwriter known for her innovative fusion of flamenco with contemporary pop, R&B, and urban music. She has revolutionized Spanish music with her unique style and powerful vocals.
