Artist Profile

About The Weeknd

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer-songwriter and record producer. Known for his sonic versatility and dark lyricism, he has become one of the most successful artists in contemporary R&B and pop music.

Musical Style

His music incorporates elements of R&B, pop, hip hop, and electronic music. He is known for his distinctive falsetto vocals and atmospheric production style.
